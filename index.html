About

“Blending soft piano motifs with expansive ambient textures — exploring stillness, cosmic space, and flowing light.”

Space Melody is an ambient music project by composer Yair Harpaz, creating atmospheric soundscapes for deep relaxation, meditation, and quiet reflection.

Each composition unfolds slowly, inviting listeners into a calm sonic environment ideal for meditation, sleep, focus, and inner balance.

The debut album Celestial Stillness drifts between piano minimalism and ethereal soundscapes — inspired by the quiet beauty of the cosmos.

Composer: Yair Harpaz
